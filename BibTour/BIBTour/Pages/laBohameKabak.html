La Bohame Kabak Hotel
Beachside Bliss: Private Beach and Shuttle Service
The hotel features a private sandy beach for exclusive guest use. La Boheme Kabak operates on a bed and breakfast basis, ensuring a delightful start to your day. Guests can also enjoy meals at the on-site à la carte restaurant or the open-air restaurant. Dive into the refreshing outdoor pool for a fun and cooling experience.
Relaxation and Recreation: Massage Services and Bar
Indulge in relaxation with massage services offered on-site, allowing you to unwind and rejuvenate. Enjoy pleasant moments at the hotel bar for a delightful time. La Boheme Kabak is conveniently located 60 km from Dalaman Airport, 25 km from Fethiye, and 12 km from Ölüdeniz.
Discover Serenity: La Boheme Kabak in Fethiye
Nestled in the captivating landscape of Ölüdeniz, La Boheme Kabak invites you to discover serenity by the sea. With its private beach, comfortable accommodations, and refreshing amenities, this hotel provides an unforgettable escape for those seeking both relaxation and adventure.
