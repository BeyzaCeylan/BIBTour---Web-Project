Authentic Central Mexico
This city-hopping adventure through the heartlands of Mexico is a love letter to a nation of ancient history, artistic sensibility and independent spirit. We’ll start in Mexico City, where epic skyscrapers live alongside ancient canal systems, while charming old neighbourhoods and bustling food markets are as vibrant as ever.
Ancient History
We’ll turn back time at the ancient sites of Teotihuacán and Tula, before embarking on a magical tour of gorgeous colonial-era cities that became the forge in which Mexican independence was fired. Unique art galleries, rural vineyards, beautiful churches and charming old cobbled streets await – not to mention a whole host of amazing foodie experiences.
Culinary Delights
Mexico City is a gastronomic paradise offering a diverse array of delicious global cuisine. Traditional taquerias, mezcalerías, and street food stalls entice those eager to explore local flavors.
Tour Highlights:
  1. Mexico City: The capital of Mexico, Mexico City, is famous for its historical richness, art galleries, museums, and vibrant culture.
  2. Teotihuacan Pyramids: Teotihuacan, an ancient civilization in Mexico, is renowned for its colossal pyramids.
  3. Chichen Itza: Located on the Yucatan Peninsula, Chichen Itza is a significant center of the Maya civilization.
  4. Playa del Carmen: Situated on the Caribbean coast, Playa del Carmen is famous for its white sandy beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife.
  5. Guanajuato: Guanajuato is renowned for its colorful houses, narrow streets, and historical richness.
