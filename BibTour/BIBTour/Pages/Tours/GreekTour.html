Greek Island Odyssey
Embrace the many facets of what makes Greece such a dreamy summer destination across nine days of city touring and island hopping with our Local Insider. In Athens you’ll get a guided tour of the Acropolis, but you’ll also discover lively modern neighbourhoods adorned with epic works of graffiti and thriving food scenes.
Sailing To Nature
We set sail for the Cyclades, heading first to Naxos, where you’ll get a glimpse into the quaint beauty of the island’s laid-back approach to life. Finally, we hop across to Santorini, home to an iconic whitewashed skyline and arguably the prettiest sunset on the planet.
The Enchantment of the Greek Islands:
The Greek Islands not only captivate with their beauty but also with their history, culture, and unique atmosphere. Each island has its own unique story and allure, making the exploration of each gem in the Aegean Sea a truly special experience.
Tour Highlights:
  1. Athens: Athens, the convergence of ancient and modern times, is famous for the historical remnants of the Acropolis, the Parthenon Temple, and the Plaka district.
  2. Santorini Island: Situated in the Aegean Sea, Santorini is famous for its whitewashed houses, stunning sunsets, and enchanting views
  3. Mykonos Island: Known for its vibrant nightlife, elegant beaches, and typical Greek island architecture, Mykonos is a popular destination among travelers.
  4. Delphi: An ancient religious center, Delphi is known for its historical ruins, the Temple of Apollo, and magnificent views.
  5. Meteora: Meteora is famous for its monasteries built on massive rock pillars.
