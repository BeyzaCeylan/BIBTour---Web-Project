Far Eastern Cuisine
Embark on an unforgettable culinary journey through the diverse and flavorful landscapes of Asia.
Sushi-Making Classes in Japan
Delve into the art of sushi-making with hands-on classes in Japan. Learn the delicate balance of flavors and techniques that create this iconic Japanese dish, from nigiri to maki rolls.
Street Food Safari in Bangkok
Embark on a street food safari in Bangkok, where the vibrant night markets come alive with an array of Thai delicacies.
Tour Highlights:
  1. Tokyo's Sushi Wonderland: Explore Tokyo's famed Tsukiji Fish Market and indulge in a sushi-making class to create traditional nigiri and rolls.
  2. Bangkok's Street Food Extravaganza: Navigate the bustling streets of Bangkok on a street food safari, tasting iconic Thai dishes like pad Thai, Som Tum, and mango sticky rice.
  3. Delhi's Spice Bazaar Adventure: Immerse yourself in the vibrant spice markets of Delhi, discovering the aromatic treasures that define Indian cuisine.
  4. Japanese Kaiseki Dining: Experience traditional kaiseki dining in Kyoto, showcasing Japanese culinary artistry.
  5. Taiwanese Night Market Adventure: Dive into Taiwan's night market, sampling local street snacks.
