Taste of France Tour
Taste of France Tour
France, a gastronomic paradise renowned for reaching the pinnacle of world cuisine, with its famous wines and unique flavors, invites food enthusiasts on an unforgettable experience through a French Food Tour.
Gastronomic Capitals of Paris
Paris, a capital in the world of gastronomy, boasts internationally acclaimed restaurants, cafes, and patisseries. The tour takes you through iconic areas such as Champs-Élysées, Montmartre, and Le Marais, offering the opportunity to savor unique flavors.
Bourgogne Wine Tours
The Bourgogne region is among the world's finest wine-producing areas. Wine tours amidst vineyards provide an excellent way to explore the wine culture of this region.
Tour Highlights:
  1. French Cooking Class: Engage in a hands-on French cooking class where participants can learn to prepare classic French dishes under the guidance of a skilled chef.
  2. Gastronomic Tour in Lyon: Explore Lyon, renowned for its gastronomic excellence.
  3. Wine Tasting in Burgundy: Embark on a wine tasting tour in the Burgundy region, known for its exceptional wines.
  4. Chocolate and Pastry Tour in Paris: Indulge in a chocolate and pastry tour in Paris, visiting renowned chocolatiers and patisseries to sample exquisite chocolates, pastries, and macarons.
  5. Cheese Tasting in Normandy: Discover the rich variety of French cheeses with a tasting session, perhaps focusing on the Camembert and Pont-l'Évêque varieties in Normandy.
