Italy Flavor Tour
Italy, the culinary heart of the Mediterranean, invites you on an unforgettable journey through its rich and diverse flavors.
Culinary Capitals
Begin your gastronomic adventure in cities like Rome, Florence, and Bologna, each boasting its own culinary identity.
Pizza Perfection in Naples
Visit the birthplace of pizza, Naples, and indulge in the authentic Neapolitan pizza experience.
Tour Highlights:
  1. Truffle Hunting in Piedmont: Explore Piedmont's forests to discover the elusive truffle, a culinary gem.
  2. Balsamic Vinegar Tasting in Modena: Experience the rich flavors of aged balsamic vinegar in Modena.
  3. Olive Oil Tasting in Tuscany: Sample Tuscany's finest olive oils and learn about the olive oil production process.
  4. Parmesan Cheese Making in Emilia-Romagna: Witness the crafting of Parmesan cheese and savor its authentic taste.
  5. Seafood Feast in Cinque Terre: Indulge in a seaside feast, savoring the fresh catch of the day in Cinque Terre.
